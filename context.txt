# NovaTech Solutions — Company Knowledge Base

## About the Company

NovaTech Solutions is a Brazilian technology company founded in 2015, headquartered in Fortaleza, Ceará.
We specialise in custom software development, cloud infrastructure management, and IT consulting for
small and medium-sized businesses across Brazil.

Our mission is to democratise access to high-quality technology solutions, helping businesses of all
sizes grow through smart digital transformation.

---

## Products and Services

### 1. Custom Software Development
- Web applications (React, Node.js, .NET)
- Mobile applications (iOS and Android — native and cross-platform with Flutter)
- API design and integration
- Legacy system modernisation

### 2. Cloud Infrastructure (NovaTech Cloud)
- Cloud migration assessment and planning
- Infrastructure setup and management on AWS, Azure, and GCP
- 24/7 infrastructure monitoring

### 3. IT Consulting
- Technology strategy roadmaps

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I start a project with NovaTech?**
A: Contact us by phone, WhatsApp, or e-mail to schedule a free 30-minute discovery call.
After that, we conduct a paid Discovery Workshop to define the scope, architecture, and budget.

**Q: Do you work with clients outside of Fortaleza?**
A: Yes! We work with clients across all of Brazil and internationally. Most meetings are
conducted remotely via video call. Our São Paulo office serves clients in the South and
Southeast regions.

**Q: How long does a typical project take?**
A: Simple web applications typically take 2–4 months. Complex enterprise systems can take
6–12 months or more. We provide a detailed timeline during the Discovery Workshop.

**Q: What technologies do you use?**
A: We work with React, Next.js, Node.js, .NET, Python, Flutter, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis,
AWS, Azure, GCP, Docker, and Kubernetes, among others. We choose the best stack for each project.

**Q: Do you offer post-launch support?**
A: Yes. We offer maintenance contracts starting at R$ 1,200/month, which includes bug fixes,
minor feature updates, and 8×5 support. Custom support packages are available for larger systems.

**Q: Is my data safe with NovaTech?**
A: Absolutely. We are LGPD compliant and follow ISO 27001-aligned security practices.
All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Our infrastructure undergoes regular security audits.

**Q: Can I try the SaaS products before buying?**
A: Yes. All three SaaS products (NovaFlow, NovaDesk, NovaSight) come with a 14-day free trial
with no credit card required. Contact our sales team to activate your trial.

**Q: How do I request a refund?**
A: For SaaS subscriptions, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee from the first payment date.
For custom projects, refund policies are defined in the individual contract.

**Q: Do you offer student or startup discounts?**
A: We have a startup programme that offers up to 30% off SaaS subscriptions for companies
under 2 years old with fewer than 10 employees. Contact our sales team for details.

**Q: How do I report a bug or get technical support?**
A: Open a ticket through the NovaDesk portal at suporte.novatechsolutions.com.br or
send an e-mail to suporte@novatechsolutions.com.br. Enterprise clients have a dedicated
Slack channel and a named technical account manager.
